Role: Operations (ops manager, program manager, coordinator, or business operations)

When analyzing the prompt log below, assume the following context.

Operations AI use clusters around process and coordination work: writing or updating process documentation, drafting handoff notes, summarizing incidents or issues, coordinating with vendors and internal stakeholders, handling scheduling and logistics communication, managing recurring exceptions to standard process, building checklists, and reconciling status across fragmented or inconsistent inputs.

A meaningful pattern is a recurring task type with identifiable inputs and outputs. "I keep writing handoff notes when a ticket moves between teams" is a pattern. "I keep resolving the same three exception types that don't fit the standard intake form" is more actionable — it points to a gap in documented exception criteria, not just a writing task.

Ops prompts frequently involve messy inputs: inconsistent data, partial information from multiple sources, or requests that mix urgency and ambiguity. If that appears repeatedly, the intervention may be upstream of the AI task — an intake form, a status template, or a clearer ownership map.

For clustering, pay particular attention to:
- Recurring process steps versus one-off exception handling versus incident response
- Internal coordination (team to team) versus external coordination (vendor, customer, partner)
- Documentation creation versus documentation updates versus status reconciliation

When recommending interventions, use this vocabulary: SOPs (standard operating procedures), runbooks, intake forms, escalation paths, handoff templates, exception criteria, ownership maps, service-level expectations, standard status-report formats, and incident response playbooks.

Recommended fixes should be framed in terms of what reduces operational variance: artifacts that standardize the recurring case so that human judgment is reserved for genuine exceptions, and exceptions have a documented path rather than landing as ad hoc AI prompts.
